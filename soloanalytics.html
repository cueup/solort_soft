Frequently Asked Questions

Want to know more about Atix Analytics cloud-hosted, user-friendly, all-in-one web analytics tool. Lightweight tracking, session replays, heatmaps, user journeys & more.


What is Atix Analytics?

Atix Analytics is a user behaviour analytics tool that helps you understand how users are interacting with your website through features such as session replays and heatmaps.

How much does it cost?

It’s FREE to try for the PRO Plan for 30 days. Charging is then based on the number of visits, heatmaps, session recordings you use per-month. Or use our FREE Plan forever.

Is it GDPR compliant?

Atix Analytics is fully GDPR complaint, it gives you the option to do privacy focused tracking or advanced tracking. Compliant with GDPR, CCPA & PECR.

  1. Supports Opt-Out for your visitors
  2. Respects DNT (Do not track)
  3. No IP addresses are being collected
